#FixMK

Meridian-Kessler's streets and sidewalks are in urgent need of repair.

Your neighbors have identified the areas of most need, and this site documents those problem areas. The Meridian-Kessler Neighborhood Association has created this site as a resource to both residents and city decision-makers.

Please view the issues by council district below. If you are interested in helping get these fixed, we ask that you contact your elected official by visiting our Take Action page. Learn more about this project at our About page.
